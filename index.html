How to Play

  • Players have 10 attempts for each stage.
  • The pikachu walls will disappear once the Poke ball hits a Pikachu.
  • Press Enter to turn on the music.
  • Press M to mute the music

    • 1. Press Enter to Start the Game

    2. Press left and/or right arrow keys to move trainer Ash left and/or right.

    3. Press up or down arrow keys to control the trajectory of the Poke Ball.

    4. Press and hold the space bar to control the power of the Poke Ball.

    5. Catch a Charmander to move onto the next stage, not Pikachu.
